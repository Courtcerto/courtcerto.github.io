Vanilla Bean

Recipe inspired by Ethan Rhode

Ingredients


  • 1 Vanilla Bean

  • 2 Cups of Brown Sugar

  • 1 Cup of Water

  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Cut the vanilla bean in half, slice the halves open and scrape out the seeds with the back of the knife.

  2. Add the sugar, water, vanilla bean seeds, and salt to a small sauch pan and bring it to a simmer over low heat. Throw in half of the vanilla bean and save the other half for Stephen's vanilla sugar. He uses the sugar to make crème brulée

  3. let simmer for 20 minutes

  4. Let syrup cool. After cooling, add to a syrup bottle. Shelf life is about 2 weeks. Enjoy!

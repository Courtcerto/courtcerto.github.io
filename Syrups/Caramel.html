Spiced Caramel

Recipe by Noah Stern

Ingredients


  • 1 cup sugar (brown)

  • 1/4 cup water

  • 2 cinnamon sticks

  • 3/4 cup heavy cream

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter

  • 1 tsp sea salt

Optional


  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg

  • 1/2 tsp cloves

  • A tiny pitch of black pepper

Instructions

  1. Melt sugar & water over medium heat until golden. Then add the cinnamon sticks and simmer for 10 minutes

  2. Carefully add cream & butter, stirring continuously.

  3. Add spices & salt.

  4. Enjoy and store in the fridge for about 2 weeks!
